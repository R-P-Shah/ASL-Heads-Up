How to Play:

  • Navigate to a category to play on the list page
  • Press a category to play the fingerspelling game
  • Hold the phone over your head and let the other player fingerspell the word to you
  • If you get it right, shake the phone upwards. If you get it wrong, shake it downwards
  • When the timer runs out, your turn is over! Check back to see how well you did.
